The Air Sausage

Description

The air sausage is considered a great delicacy among the sausage community and the creation of one is a show of the chef's mastery.
While cooking the sausage, the chef needs to be extremely cautious to not pop the sausage casing.

Ingredients

  • High Quality Sausage Casing
  • Functioning Lungs
  • 2 Tablespoons Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Fill the sausage casing like it's a balloon and make sure to tie the open end
  2. Put the oil and the sausage onto a pan and put your stove on high heat
  3. Cook the sausage until you see some discoloration
  4. Make sure to take the sausage off the heat immediately after
  5. Depending on your preference you can either present it with a bun or by itself
