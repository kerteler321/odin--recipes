Water Sausage

Description

The water sausage is the national dish of South Dakota.
It is considered as a symbol of freedom. It depicts the idea, that you can do anything you dream of.

Ingredients

  • High Quality Sausage Casing
  • A Functioning Sink
  • Two Tablespoons Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Start by filling the casing with water and tie the end of the casing
  2. For this one first we'll need to put the sausage in boiling water for about 3 minutes
  3. After 3 minutes take the sausage out of the boiling water and put the oil onto a pan
  4. Cook the sausage on the pan using high heat
  5. Be careful not to pop the sausage and take it out as soon as you see discoloration
  6. Your water sausage is done! You can present it either by itself or inside a bun
